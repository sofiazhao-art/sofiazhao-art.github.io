Sofia Zhao / 赵冰冰

Bingbing Zhao, also known as Sofia Zhao, is a Chinese artist currently working across painting, mixed media, and works on paper. Her work moves between figuration and abstraction, focusing on the unstable relationship between inner experience and external structures.

Prior to dedicating herself to her current painting practice, Zhao studied and worked in Japan within highly structured professional environments, including IT and consulting-related fields. Her early experience in illustration and visual storytelling served as an important starting point for her image-making; notably, her illustration work The Half was longlisted for the Bologna Illustrators Exhibition. These formative experiences continue to shape her sensitivity to order, efficiency, regulation, institutional language, and the invisible constraints embedded in social structures.

赵冰冰，亦以 Sofia Zhao 为英文名，是一位中国艺术家，现阶段主要从事架上绘画、混合媒介与纸本创作。她的作品游走于具象与抽象之间，关注个体内部经验与外在结构之间不稳定的关系。

在投入现阶段的绘画实践之前，赵曾在日本学习与工作，并置身于高度结构化的职业环境之中，包括 IT 与咨询相关领域。早期的插画与视觉叙事经验，也构成了她进入图像创作的重要起点；其插画作品《The Half》曾入围 Bologna Illustrators Exhibition 长名单。这些形成性经历持续塑造着她对秩序、效率、规训、制度化语言，以及社会结构中隐性限制的敏感。
